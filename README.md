# fom-sdk

This repository is, currently, a grouping of files describing methods of editing and modifying *Fields of Mistria.*
We do not provide customer support for modding, nor do we recommend altering your game files. Anything you do to your own
game you do at your own risk, including destroying saves, damaging your own computer, or any other consequences.

**If you want to play the game without complications, simply play Fields of Mistria.** You can buy it on [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2142790/Fields_of_Mistria/).

To see how to add objects and items, [read here](./CREATING_OBJECTS_AND_ITEMS.md).
